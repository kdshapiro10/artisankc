About Artisan General Contractors

Artisan General Contracting is a newly established general contracting firm specializing in multifamily and light industrial construction. While our company is young, our expertise is rooted in the extensive experience of our founding partners, David Shapiro, and John Gaar. Together, they bring decades of proven success in the construction industry, blending innovative solutions with time-honored craftsmanship to deliver superior results.

Mission Statement

"Our mission at Artisan General Contracting is to build exceptional, sustainable, and innovative multifamily communities that enrich lives and elevate the standard of living. Through our expertise in construction and architecture, we are committed to delivering high- quality projects that meet the diverse needs of our clients while enhancing the fabric of the neighborhoods we serve. We prioritize integrity, collaboration, and excellence in every aspect of our work, ensuring that each development is not only a place to live but a place to thrive."
